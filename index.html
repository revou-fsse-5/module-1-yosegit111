The Indonesian Embassy in Singapore held Eid al-Fitr prayers 1 Syawal 1445 Hijriah


The Indonesian Embassy in Singapore held Eid al-Fitr prayers 1 Syawal 1445 Hijriah, Wednesday, 10 April 2024, at the premise of the Indonesian Embassy in Singapore. The prayer held at 08.00 local time was solemn, and was attended by almost 3500 Indonesian citizens who live in Singapore, including the Indonesian Ambassador to Singapore, Mr. Suryo Pratomo and his family and the Deputy Ambassador of the Republic of Indonesia, Mr. Sultistijo Djati Ismojo and his family.
